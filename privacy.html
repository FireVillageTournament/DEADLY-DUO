Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Fire Village Deadly Duo Free Fire Tournament website. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: When registering for the tournament, we may collect your team name, contact number, and (optionally) your team logo.
  • Payment Details: Entry fees are paid via UPI/GPay. We do not store your payment details, but we may collect your payment confirmation screenshots for verification.
  • Social Media: If you contact us via Instagram, WhatsApp, or Facebook, we may view your public profile and conversation history necessary for registration.

How We Use Your Information

  • To register your team and manage tournament participation.
  • For communication related to match timings, schedules, and results.
  • To verify payments and confirm your spot in the tournament.
  • To announce winners and share tournament updates on our social media and YouTube channel (team names and logos may be displayed).
  • We never sell or share your personal data with third parties except as required for tournament operations.

Your Rights

  • You may request deletion of your data after the tournament by contacting us on Instagram at @firevillage.in.
  • You can request information on what data we hold about you at any time.

Security

We take appropriate steps to ensure your data is protected and used only for tournament purposes.

Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as necessary. The latest version will always be available on this page.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please DM us on Instagram: @firevillage.in
